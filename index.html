The Best Recreation Park

Because we all deserve unique things

how is?
The state of Chiapas has been characterized as a state with many green areas, and in Tuxtla Gutierrez Chiapas, the state capital, has green spaces and some of the most representative have been the Caña Hueca Recreational Park and the Joyu Mayu Park. They are located in the north west part of the city, it is a very accessible place and easy to reach with public transport. This park is one of the most beautiful and most visited in the city, since it is a place with vegetation, which allows you to have very beautiful settings to take a photo. They are places of coexistence and family. Since they are adapted to carry out many recreational activities and practice sports, their facilities include: cafeteria areas basketball courts, Soccer fields and grass fast soccer field Tennis court Fronton-tennis court Apparatus and equipment to exercise properly Areas for Aerobics, Spinning, Outdoor Gym and Medical Assistance. These spaces are excellent places to relax and have a good time of healthy fun, they are undoubtedly places worth visiting.
